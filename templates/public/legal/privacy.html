{% translate "Who we are" %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} This service matches partners for the 4 Vallées Ambassadors Program. The data controller for the personal data processed here is Groupe Télé-Thyon SA. For privacy questions, contact caissier@tele-thyon.ch. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "What we collect" %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} We deliberately collect only what matchmaking and contact require: your name, email address, phone number, your role (Ambassador or Referee), your chosen price category, your prior-season eligibility attestation, and your preferred resort. We do not collect the full application details (date of birth, address, photo ID, keycard, insurance) — those are handled off-app when you apply in person. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Why we use it" %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} We use your data to place you in the matching queue, pair you with a partner, and let you contact each other once you have both accepted a match. Your email and phone are never shown to another participant before you have both accepted. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "How long we keep it" %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} We keep your data for the duration of the season you registered for and a short period afterwards, then delete it. You can delete your account at any time from your account page. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Your rights (GDPR / Swiss FADP)" %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. You can exercise most of these directly from your account page, or by contacting us. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} This policy is a first-draft baseline and should be reviewed by a legal professional before the program launches. {% endblocktranslate %}
